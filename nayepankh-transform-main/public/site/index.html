About Us

Wings of possibility for every young dreamer.

NayePankh Foundation is a youth-focused non-profit dedicated to bridging the gap between education and opportunity. We equip students and early-career professionals with practical skills, industry mentorship, and community projects that create lasting impact.

Mission

Empower one million young minds with the tools, mentorship, and confidence to lead meaningful change.

Vision

A world where every young person, regardless of background, has wings to fly toward their potential.
